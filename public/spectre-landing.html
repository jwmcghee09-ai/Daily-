What the AI Actually Reads

Live data + quant simulations fed directly into every response

Before answering, the AI ingests your full portfolio state — every holding's weight, cost base, unrealised P&L, sector, and live price — alongside a real-time market snapshot and the output of institutional-grade quant models. It's not searching the web. It's reading your actual numbers.

  • Live ASX, crypto, and ETF prices at query time
  • VIX level and current market regime classification
  • Monte Carlo simulation paths (10,000+) for portfolio projection
  • Cornish-Fisher adjusted VaR and Expected Shortfall (CVaR)
  • Factor exposures — market beta, size tilt via ASX Small Ords
  • Holding correlation matrix across top positions
  • Concentration risk, HHI score, and sector allocation
  • Max drawdown, volatility, and trailing risk window metrics
  • Top movers and session P&L across your positions
